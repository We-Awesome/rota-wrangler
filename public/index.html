Feature list

  • Create & share rotas

    Our rota management platform is efficient and simple to use. We designed it with you and your business in mind because we understand how important your time is and want you to spend it focused on achieving your goals.

  • Setup departments & roles

    Setup departments within your business and add staff by roles. Rota Wrangler will design your dashboard and group your team by department, so you don’t have to.

  • Manage time off

    Add holiday allowance and let your staff request their own time off. Managers can easily review and approve or deny requests with visibility of all staff absences. We make scheduling and approving holiday whilst on the go quick and easy.

  • Notify team members

    Rotas can be easily shared with your teams in one just one click of a button. Staff will receive an email informing them of their shifts for the week/month ahead, making printing rotas a thing of the past.

  • Swap shifts easily

    Take the stress out of last minute shift changes and let your staff handle shift swapping. If they can’t make the shift, then give them the power to organise their own cover. There is no need to make lots of phone calls, or send texts to every team member.

  • Available on all devices

    Our platform is mobile friendly and available on all devices. Rotas can be accessed anytime, anywhere ensuring easy, up to date team management from any location.
